# Heartbeat

Runs every weekday morning before [USER_NAME] starts work.

## Checklist

1. Check calendar for today's events — note back-to-backs or conflicts
2. Load tasks/current.md — surface today's items, flag anything overdue
3. Check work inbox ([WORK_EMAIL]) — surface anything urgent
4. Check personal inbox ([PERSONAL_EMAIL]) — surface bills, appointments, anything requiring action
5. Check reminders/active.md — surface anything due today or tomorrow
6. Check habits/log.md — note if yesterday's habits were logged
7. Check shopping/list.md — surface any errands marked urgent

## Output format

**Good morning. Here's your day.**

📅 **Calendar** — [N events. Flag conflicts.]
✅ **Tasks** — [Top 3 for today. Flag overdue.]
📬 **Inbox** — [Work: X items. Personal: Y items.]
🔔 **Reminders** — [Anything due today or tomorrow.]
🛒 **Errands** — [Urgent shopping list items.]
💪 **Habits** — [Yesterday's log status.]
